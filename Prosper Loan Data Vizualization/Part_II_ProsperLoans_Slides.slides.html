Investigation Overview¶

Presentation Objectives:

  1. What the dataset is made up of & the distribution of the variables in the dataset.

  2. Assess the relationships between the variables in the dataset

  3. Which variable influence the Prosper Score and Interest Rate APR.

Dataset Overview¶

The data consists of information about 113,937 loans granted by Prosper. This includes the Prosper score, listing category (loan type), employment status, number of current credit lines, number of open revolving accounts, debt to income ratio, and original loan amount. The list is extensive.

Overview: Loan Category & Application Employment Status¶

Most applications for loans were for Debt Consolidation

Majority of the applicants were employed and earning an income

Employment Status On Interest Rate APR¶

Applicants that are employed receive a slightly higher loan amount

While Employment status does not have a strong influence on the interest rate, not being employed will result in a higher interest rate

Loan Amount & Interest Rate APR & Home Owner Relationship¶

Larger loans are more likely to have lower intrest rates

While smaller loans are more variable with the interest rates

There is no relationship with homeowners getting better interest rates, however more homeowners received larger sized loans
